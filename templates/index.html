Tips for Better Recognition

  • Ensure good, consistent lighting. Avoid shadows on your hands and face.
  • Position yourself so your upper body and hands are clearly visible.
  • Wear clothing that contrasts with your skin tone if possible.
  • Perform signs clearly and at a natural pace.
  • After signing with webcam, return to a neutral, hands-down position before clicking "Stop".
  • Minimize background distractions.
